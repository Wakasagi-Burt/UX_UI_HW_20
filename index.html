CHAT BOX

Chat Box was an app my team and I designed with the vision of helping those who needed a little more structure in their lives. We felt that with so many platforms to message on it can be a bit overwhelming. Chat Box can help centralize and prioritize any and all of your messages from different platforms from your phone, or desktop computer.

My role in this project was to help interview possible users and create our teams proto-persona. From there I our team wireframed our low fidelity interface. I created our first iteration of our mid fidelity prototype. The whole team colalborrated thereafter to create our final design.
